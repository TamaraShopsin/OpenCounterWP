What are Business Improvement Assessments?

Your Business Improvement Assessments are spent exclusively on promoting and improving the downtown area. The Assessments are collected by the City of Santa Cruz and forwarded directly to the Downtown Association of Santa Cruz. Learn more about the Downtown Association of Santa Cruz.
